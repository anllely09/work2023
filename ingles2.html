Symptoms and Diseases

1. An allergy: A negative reaction to something you eat or touch.

Una alergia: Una reacción negativa a algo que comes o tocas.

John has an allergy.

- He should avoid contact with the allergen.


2. Asthma: Respiratory problem. Difficulty of breathing.

Asma: Problema respiratorio. Dificultad para respirar.

Camila has an asthma attack.

- Why don’t she use an inhaler?


3. A backache: A prolonged pain in the back.

Un dolor de espalda: Un dolor prolongado en la espalda.

Gloria has a backache.

- How about correcting your posture?


4. A broken leg: A bone in the leg is broken.

Una pierna rota: Un hueso en la pierna está roto.

David has a broken leg.

- How about going to the hospital?


5. A cough: The act of expelling air from the lungs with a sudden sharp sound.

Tos: El acto de expulsar el aire de los pulmones con un sonido agudo repentino.

Simon has a cough.

- He should drink a of fluids.


6. A cold: A common viral infection which causes mucus to run from the nose, gives a sore throat

often includes sneezing.

Un resfriado: Una infección viral común que hace que la mucosidad salga de la nariz, produce dolor

de garganta y a menudo incluye estornudos.

Pedro has a cold.

- He should take a syrup.


7. An earache: Pain inside the ear.

Un dolor de oído: Dolor dentro del oído.

Sofia has a earache.

- Why don’t she go to the otolaryngologist?


8. A fever: An abnormally high body temperature, usually accompanied by shivering and a headache.

Fiebre: Una temperatura corporal anormalmente alta, generalmente acompañada de escalofríos y dolor de

cabeza.

Elizabeth has a fever.

- Why don’t she take a warm shower?


9. The flu: Is the common name given for influenza. It is a contagious viral infection of the respiratory

passages that causes fever and sever aching.

La gripe: Es ese nombre común que se le da a la influenza. Es una infección viral contagiosa de las vías

respiratorias que causa fiebre y dolor severo.

Maria has the flu

- She should rest


10. A headache: A continuous pain in the head.

Un dolor de cabeza: Un dolor continuo en la cabeza.

Alexandra has a headache.

She should take a pill.


11. Heartburn: A form of indigestion felt as a burning sensation in the chest.

Acidez: Una forma de indigestión que se siente como una sensación de ardor en el pecho.

Thomas has a heartburn.

How about stop eating excess food?


12. A rash: A lot of small red spots on the skin that are usually itchy.

Una erupción: Muchas pequeñas manchas rojas en la piel que generalmente pican.

Luciana has a rash.

Why don’t she apply lotion?


13. A sore throat: A condition of pain in the throat, typically caused by inflammation of it.

Un dolor de garganta: Una condición de dolor en la garganta, generalmente causa una inflamación en la misma.

Alejandro has a sore throat.

How about avoiding wearing down your throat?


14. A sprain: An injury to a joint in your body, especially your wrist or ankle, caused by suddenly twisting it.

Un esguince: Una lesión en una articulación del cuerpo, especialmente en la muñeca o el tobillo, causada por

torcerla repentinamente.

Mario has a sprain.

Why don’t he apply ice to reduce inflammation?


15. A stomachache: The pain in a person’s belly.

Un dolor de estómago: El dolor en el vientre de una persona.

Jose has a stomachache.

How about avoiding hard to digest foods?


16. A toothache: The pain in a tooth or teeth.

Un dolor de muelas: Un dolor en un diente o dientes.

Samantha has a toothache.

She should go to the dentist.
